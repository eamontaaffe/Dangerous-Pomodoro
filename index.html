This is a mash up of a pomodoro clock and the most dangerous writing app. Basically the idea is, as soon as you start working you cant really stop for a full pomodoro (25 minutes) because if you stop for more than 30 seconds, I am going to delete all your work. haha

There is a save button, but it is only activated at the end of a pomodoro session. So you better keep working!
