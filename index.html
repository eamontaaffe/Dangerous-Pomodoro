0:05


This is a mash up of a pomodoro clock and the most dangerous writing app by @maebert. The idea is as soon as you start working you can't stop for a full pomodoro (25 minutes) because if you stop for more than 30 seconds, I am going to delete all your work. haha-haha-haha-haaaaa.

There is a save button, but it is only activated at the end of a pomodoro session. So you better keep working!

After every pomodoro, you can have a 5 minute break. At the end of every 4th pomodoro, treat yourself to a full 30 minute break because you deserve it.
